1. Tadasana (Mountain Pose):

Benefits: Improves posture, balance, and body awareness.

Advantages: Foundation for all standing poses, strengthens core.

Symptoms: Poor posture, body aches, lack of coordination.

2. Adho Mukha Svanasana (Downward-Facing Dog):

Benefits: Stretches hamstrings, calves, spine, and shoulders, strengthens core and upper body.

Advantages: Improves circulation, energizes the body, relieves back pain.

Symptoms: Tight hamstrings, back pain, fatigue, poor circulation.

3. Vrikshasana (Tree Pose):

Benefits: Improves balance, focus, and concentration.

Advantages: Strengthens ankles and core, promotes mental clarity.

Symptoms: Poor balance, anxiety, difficulty focusing.

4. Balasana (Child's Pose):

Benefits: Releases tension in the lower back, neck, and shoulders, promotes relaxation.

Advantages: Restorative pose, calms the mind, aids digestion.

Symptoms: Stress, anxiety, back pain, neck pain, digestive issues.

5. Marjariasana (Cat-Cow Pose):

Benefits: Stretches and strengthens the spine, improves flexibility.

Advantages: Gentle movement for the spine, improves circulation.

Symptoms: Stiff back, neck pain, poor posture.

6. Bhujangasana (Cobra Pose):

Benefits: Opens the chest and shoulders, strengthens the back.

Advantages: Improves posture, energizes the body, alleviates back pain (gentle modification needed).

Symptoms: Rounded shoulders, hunchback posture, mild back pain.

7. Setu Bandhasana (Bridge Pose):

Benefits: Strengthens the hamstrings, glutes, and core, opens the chest.

Advantages: Improves flexibility, promotes relaxation, reduces fatigue.

Symptoms: Tight hamstrings, weak core, fatigue.

8. Utthita Trikonasana (Triangle Pose):

Benefits: Stretches the sides of the body, improves hip and leg flexibility.

Advantages: Strengthens core and legs, improves balance

Symptoms: Tight hips, leg stiffness, poor balance.

9. Running:

Benefits: Promotes deep relaxation, reduces stress and anxiety.

Advantages: Restorative pose, improves sleep quality, promotes mindfulness.

Symptoms: Stress, anxiety, insomnia, tension headaches.
